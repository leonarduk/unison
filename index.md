## UNISoN - UseNet Incorporates Social Networks

### About UNISoN

UNISoN is a Java-based NNTP client that can analyse messages to save to a Pajek-format file for Social Network Analysis. Developed as part of an MSc Business Systems Analysis & Design at City University in 2008, it was made Open Source in 2016.
